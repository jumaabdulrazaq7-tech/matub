About Us

About MATUB

MATUB Foodstuffs Trading Co LLC is a trusted food trading company based in the United Arab Emirates. We specialize in sourcing, exporting, and distributing premium-quality agricultural commodities and food products across international markets.

Building Global Connections

Founded with a vision to connect quality agricultural producers with international markets, MATUB has grown into a trusted name in food trading. Our headquarters in the UAE positions us strategically to serve clients across the Middle East, Asia, Africa, and Europe.

We work closely with certified farms and processing facilities to ensure that every product meets international standards. Our team of experienced professionals oversees every aspect of the supply chain, from sourcing and quality control to logistics and documentation.

Whether you are looking for fresh produce, grains, spices, or dried foods, MATUB is your reliable partner for consistent quality and dependable service.
